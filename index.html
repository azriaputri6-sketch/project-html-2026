HELLO IT'S ME

AZRIA PUTRI

I come from Bacan, South Halmahera, and I was born on March 10, 2007. I am currently studying at Sam Ratulangi University (UNSRAT), majoring in Informatics Engineering. At this stage of my life, I am still learning many things and trying to understand more about myself, my interests, and the path that I want to pursue in the future.

I am a person who can be quite shy and sometimes finds it difficult to start conversations, especially in new situations or with people I am not familiar with. Even so, I can still socialize well and connect with others, especially when I feel comfortable and understood. Although I may seem quiet at first, I actually care deeply and enjoy meaningful connections with people around me.

I also realize that one of my habits is procrastinating, and it is something I truly want to change. Sometimes I delay things, overthink too much, or feel stuck in my own process. There are moments when I compare myself to others and wonder if I am moving too slowly. However, I am learning that growth does not always have to be fast, and that every person has their own pace in life.

Even with my weaknesses and doubts, I still try to stay strong, hold on to my values, and continue moving forward little by little. I believe that becoming a better person is a process, and I want to keep improving myself, both personally and academically. Right now, I may still be figuring things out, but I know that I want to keep learning, growing, and becoming someone I can be proud of.

"Still learning, still growing, and slowly becoming the person I want to be."
